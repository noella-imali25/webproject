About Us

Lunar Express Technology Corp. a.k.a LuEx is a Kenyan private aerospace manufacturer, space transportation services and communications company headquartered in Mombasa, Kenya. LuEx was founded in 2021 by Kalutu Daniel with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of the Moon.

LuEx's achievements include the first privately funded liquid-propellant rocket to reach orbit (Lunar Orbiter Launch Vehicle-1 a.k.a LOLv-1) and the first private company to build a spacecraft with a special Cryogenic Fuel (Lunexagen) , that has increased the thrust by 1.39x times in comparison with today's conventional spacecrafts. Currently LuEx is developing next generation Space Craft for Affordable, Reusable and Reliable inter-planetary communication.
